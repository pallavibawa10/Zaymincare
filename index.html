What We Offer

Support services built around your life

Every service we provide is designed to meet you where you are, supporting your goals, your independence and your sense of belonging in the community.

Assist Access
We help people find and access the right services, making the whole process less overwhelming and more empowering.
Assist – Life Stage
Guidance and support through key life transitions, helping participants plan and navigate changes with clarity and confidence.
Assist – Personal Activities
Hands-on daily living support with dignity at the core. From personal care to household tasks, always on your terms.
Assist – Travel & Transport
Reliable, safe transport support to help participants get to appointments, activities, and community events across Adelaide.
Community Nursing Care
Skilled nursing support delivered in the community, where people feel most comfortable. Health care that comes to you, with warmth and continuity.
Daily Tasks
Support with the everyday tasks that keep life running smoothly, so you can focus on what matters most to you.
Innovative Community Participation
We open doors to community life through creative, tailored programs. Real skills, real friendships, real belonging.
Development – Life Skills
Building practical life skills that support greater independence. Things like cooking, budgeting, communication and self-care.
Participate Community
Encouraging active participation in social, recreational and civic life. Belonging is a fundamental human need and we help make it real.
Specialised Supported Employment
We help people find work that fits and stick with it. The right adjustments, the right support and someone genuinely in your corner.
Group & Centre Activities
Group activities and centre programs designed to be genuinely enjoyable. A place to make friends, learn new things and feel part of something.

Our Story

Built from friendship, purpose, and lived experience

Zaymin did not start in a boardroom. It started in a classroom, between two women who saw the gaps in disability support firsthand and decided to do something about it.

PB
Pallavi Bawa
Co-Founder & Director

Pallavi is an educator in disability support services with deep expertise in Certificate III and IV in Individual Support. She brings the knowledge of how great care is built, structured and sustained.

📞 0412 492 112
NN
Nancy Narula
Co-Founder & Director

Nancy worked as a support worker herself, which means she brings something most directors never have: a real feel for what participants actually need day to day. She has been in the room. She knows.

📞 0424 316 534
"

Pallavi and Nancy met while studying Certificate III in Individual Support. Two women from different professional backgrounds, united by a shared belief that people with disabilities deserve far better than what was available. Through their studies and work in the sector, they saw the same gaps again and again: support that felt transactional, care that lacked consistency, and a system that too often forgot the person at the centre of it all.

Their answer was Zaymin Disability Services. Not a provider built on paperwork and process, but one grounded in genuine relationships, deep expertise and an unwavering commitment to dignified, person-centred care. Together, they bring something rare to this industry. Pallavi knows how good care is taught and structured. Nancy knows how it feels to deliver it every day. That combination is what makes Zaymin different.
